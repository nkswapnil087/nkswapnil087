# 💫 About Me:
I am a Computer Science student at the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) with a deep-seated passion for Cybersecurity. Currently, I’m developing Guardian Link, a management system for childcare organizations, where I apply my interests in secure architecture and data flow.<br><br>My focus is on mastering the 'protect and break' cycle, studying Cryptography to secure data and Web Vulnerabilities to understand modern threats. I'm an active learner in authentication and authorization labs and a firm believer in the power of community, whether it's through CTFs or helping others just starting their security journey. When I'm away from the terminal, you’ll likely find me on a cricket pitch or behind a camera lens capturing the action.
